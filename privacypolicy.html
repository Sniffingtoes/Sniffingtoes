Jambo Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication and profile management. Upon launching the Game, we access the following information: Your public display name and your unique Meta user ID.

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

To provide a seamless experience, Jambo collects data regarding: In-game progress, achievements, and unlocked content; Avatar customizations and cosmetic preferences; Transaction history for in-game currency or items; Technical diagnostics (OS version, device model, and performance logs).

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your information to ensure Jambo remains functional and fair. Specifically, this data is used to: Operate, maintain, and optimize the Game environment; Diagnose technical glitches, crashes, and performance bottlenecks; Uphold community standards and enforce anti-cheat protocols; Facilitate player support and resolve account inquiries; Refine gameplay balance based on user interaction patterns.

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data to third parties or advertisers. We only share information with: Service Providers and Legal Necessity.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary to support your gameplay experience or fulfill legal requirements. Should your Meta account be deactivated or your access to Jambo terminate, your associated data will be permanently deleted or scrubbed of all identifying markers (anonymized).

5. Protection of Minors

Jambo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of any such data collection without verified parental consent, it will be purged from our servers immediately.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access or loss. While no digital platform can guarantee absolute security, we continuously monitor and update our systems to mitigate risks.

7. Policy Revisions

As Jambo grows, we may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date." For major updates, we will notify players through prominent in-game announcements.
