Jambo Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication. Upon launching the Game, we access:

  • Your public display name
  • Your unique Meta user ID

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

We collect data regarding progress, achievements, customizations, and technical diagnostics.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

This data is used to: Operate the Game environment, diagnose technical glitches, uphold community standards, and facilitate support.

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data. We only share information with trusted service providers or for legal necessity.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary. Deactivated Meta account data will be permanently deleted.

5. Protection of Minors

Jambo is for users 13+. We do not knowingly collect info from children under 13.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access.

7. Policy Revisions

As Jambo grows, we may update this Policy. Major updates will be notified in-game.
