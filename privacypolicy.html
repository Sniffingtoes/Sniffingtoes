Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration: Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication and profile management. Upon launching the Game, we access your public display name and unique Meta user ID.

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics: To provide a seamless experience, Jambo collects data regarding in-game progress, achievements, cosmetic preferences, transaction history, and technical diagnostics.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your information to ensure Jambo remains functional and fair. This includes maintenance, diagnosing glitches, anti-cheat protocols, and player support.

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data. We only share information with essential service providers or when legally required.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary. Upon account deactivation, data is permanently deleted or anonymized.

5. Protection of Minors

Jambo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access.

7. Policy Revisions

As Jambo grows, we may update this policy. Major updates will be announced in-game.
