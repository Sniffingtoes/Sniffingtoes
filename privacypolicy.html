Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection & Sources

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication. Upon launching the Game, we access:

1.1 Meta Account Integration

  • Your public display name
  • Your unique Meta user ID

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

  • In-game progress and unlocked achievements
  • Avatar and cosmetic preferences
  • Technical diagnostics and performance logs

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your information to ensure Jambo remains functional and fair:

  • Maintain and optimize the Game environment
  • Diagnose glitches and crashes
  • Enforce anti-cheat protocols

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data. Information is only shared with service providers for game operations or for legal necessity.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Upon account deactivation, data is permanently deleted or anonymized.
