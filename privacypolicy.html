Privacy Policy

Ensuring your data remains private and secure while you play Jambo.

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication and profile management. Upon launching the Game, we access the following information:

  • Your public display name
  • Your unique Meta user ID

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

To provide a seamless experience, Jambo collects data regarding:

  • In-game progress, achievements, and unlocked content
  • Avatar customizations and cosmetic preferences
  • Transaction history for in-game currency or items
  • Technical diagnostics (OS version, device model, and performance logs)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your information to ensure Jambo remains functional and fair. Specifically, this data is used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and optimize the Game environment
  • Diagnose technical glitches and performance bottlenecks
  • Uphold community standards and enforce anti-cheat protocols
  • Facilitate player support and resolve account inquiries
  • Refine gameplay balance based on interaction patterns

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data to third parties or advertisers. We only share information with:

Service Providers: Trusted backend and analytics partners essential for game operations.

Legal Necessity: Authorities or regulatory bodies when required by law or to ensure community safety.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary to support your gameplay experience. If your Meta account is deactivated, associated data will be permanently deleted or anonymized.

5. Protection of Minors

Jambo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Any such data collection will be purged immediately upon discovery.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard technical safeguards to protect your information. While no digital platform is 100% secure, we continuously monitor and update our systems.

7. Policy Revisions

As Jambo grows, we may update this policy. Major updates will be notified through prominent in-game announcements.
