Jambo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication and profile management. Upon launching the Game, we access:

  • Your public display name
  • Your unique Meta user ID

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

To provide a seamless experience, Jambo collects data regarding:

  • In-game progress, achievements, and unlocked content
  • Avatar customizations and cosmetic preferences
  • Transaction history for in-game currency or items
  • Technical diagnostics (OS version, device model, and performance logs)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your information to ensure Jambo remains functional and fair. This data is used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and optimize the Game environment
  • Diagnose technical glitches, crashes, and performance bottlenecks
  • Uphold community standards and enforce anti-cheat protocols
  • Facilitate player support and resolve account inquiries
  • Refine gameplay balance based on user interaction patterns

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data to third parties or advertisers. We only share information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted backend and analytics partners essential for game operations.
  • Legal Necessity: Authorities when required by law or to ensure community safety.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary to support your gameplay or fulfill legal requirements. Should your Meta account be deactivated, your associated data will be permanently deleted or anonymized.
