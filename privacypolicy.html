Jambo Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Sources

1.1 Meta Account Integration

Jambo utilizes Meta accounts for secure authentication and profile management. Upon launching the Game, we access the following information: Your public display name and your unique Meta user ID.

1.2 Gameplay & Analytics

To provide a seamless experience, Jambo collects data regarding: In-game progress, achievements, avatar customizations, transaction history, and technical diagnostics.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Specifically, this data is used to: Operate and optimize the Game environment, diagnose technical glitches, uphold community standards, and facilitate player support.

3. Information Disclosure

Jambo does not sell user data to third parties. We only share information with essential service providers or for legal necessity.

4. Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for the duration necessary. Should your Meta account be deactivated, associated data will be permanently deleted or anonymized.

5. Protection of Minors

Jambo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard technical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access.

7. Policy Revisions

As Jambo grows, we may update this Privacy Policy. Major updates will be notified through in-game announcements.
